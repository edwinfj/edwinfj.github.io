Coding is an addictive for me

2016-03-04
#coding #fun #life

I finished my work. I closed my computer. I left the office.

I had my dinner. I went back home. I opened my Mac.

I started coding. Time flys. Three hours passed. I did not notice.

I enjoyed every second, but I have to sleep now.

Wish I had more hours.

It's time looking forward to tomorrow's coming, another wonderful day ahead.
